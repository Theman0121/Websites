About T (Founder)

Hello! My name is Theman (T for short). I am the founder of Spy Financial. I have been in the cryptocurrency space since Q2 2020 and throughout my time in the space I have gained fascination for all things Web3. I spent my first year exploring cryptocurrencies and began exploring crypto mining. I started mining with LoRaWAN miners then made my way toward GPU mining Ethereum and CPU mining Monero. I also have a couple other miners that mine using a different consensus aside from Proof-of-Work and Proof-of-Coverage.
As I dove deeper into Web3 I began to discover nodes which brought me to the NFT community and where I am today.
My fascination with Web3, coupled with my interest in crypto mining, NFTs, and community togetherness led me to the creation of Spy Financial.

About Life Eternal (Business Dev.)

I am a Former US Marine, did one tour and was medically discharged. After that I took some time to get myself together and joined in the professional gaming scene in WoW. During that time I started up a business fixing and repairing computers, custom builds and circuits for specific applications. I worked with an antenna/satellite company helping them upgrade their infrastructure.

I became tired of the rat race and unvalued nature of staffing and realized, through their work at home program, I might as well just do things myself again.
I have been a long term investor in stocks and precious metals, started mining crypto in roughly 2013, Invested into land in late 2017 and now rent that to be farmed. I joined more crypto in a basic "retail investor" sense in 2018 coin trading because it felt more like stocks.  Around 2019 I shifted more towards NFT's and DeFi in 2020.

I am involved as a business dev/right hand man for a good few protocols across several chains and consult for many others. I invest in anything I am a part of so my success is directly related to what I provide to the ecosystem.
